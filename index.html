 Chelsea FC

Team Photo 25/26

Chelsea Football Club is a professional football team based in London, England, founded in 1905. The club plays its home matches at Stamford Bridge, a historic stadium in West London. Known as "The Blues" due to their iconic blue kits, Chelsea has become one of the most successful and recognizable clubs in both English and European football.

Over the years, Chelsea has won numerous major trophies, including multiple Premier League titles, FA Cups, and UEFA Champions League titles. The club rose to global prominence in the 2000s, especially following the takeover by Russian billionaire Roman Abramovich in 2003, which ushered in a new era of investment and success.

Today, Chelsea continues to compete at the highest levels of football, with a strong fan base worldwide and a reputation for both talent development and international star power.
