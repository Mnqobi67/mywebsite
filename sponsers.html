Sponsers

Major / Global Partners

  • Nike – Chelsea’s kit supplier from the 2017/18 season onward, under a long‑term deal.

  • Cadbury – Official chocolate partner; works with Chelsea’s foundation on small‑business and inclusion initiatives.

  • Sure (a brand of Unilever) – Official deodorant/antiperspirant partner (known as Rexona globally); renewed in 2023 with a two‑year agreement.

  • Linglong Tire – Named as a global partner in 2024, official tyre partner of Chelsea, including the team bus and global branding activation.

  • Predator Energy – Official Energy Drinks Partner & Official Hydration Partner (announced September 2024). 

  • FPT Corporation – Technology solutions provider; global partnership announced April 2025 to help Chelsea enhance digital and fan‑engagement operations. 

  • Carling – Multi‑year partnership announced June 2025 with Molson Coors’ Carling brand as the club’s official beer partner for 2025/26 onward.
